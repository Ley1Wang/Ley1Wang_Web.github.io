Radar Detector Project

A 360-degree radar platform based on Arduino Uno, mmWave radar sensing, slip-ring transmission and real-time visualization software.

This project explores low-cost radar sensing and real-time target visualization using Arduino-based hardware and mmWave radar technology. The goal is to build a complete 360° scanning platform capable of detecting and displaying nearby objects.
